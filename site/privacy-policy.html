ROFLFaucet Privacy Policy

TL;DR: We don't care about your privacy, but if you do, read on. We collect minimal data (just usernames for game stats) and don't need any real information from you.

What We Collect

  • Usernames: For game statistics, leaderboards, and personal progress tracking
  • Game Data: Your game results, bet amounts, winnings (tied to your username)
  • Browser Data: Standard web server logs (IP addresses, browser types, page visits)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Real names, addresses, phone numbers, or any personal information
  • Email addresses (unless you voluntarily provide them)
  • Financial information or payment details
  • Tracking cookies for advertising purposes

How We Use Your Data

  • Game Statistics: Show your personal stats, create leaderboards, track achievements
  • Site Analytics: Understand which games are popular, optimize user experience
  • Public Display: Usernames may appear in leaderboards and high scores (they're meant to be public!)

Your Choices

  • Play as Guest: Don't create an account - your stats will be tracked as "guest" only
  • Use a Pseudonym: Pick any username you want - we don't verify identities
  • Clear Your Data: Contact us to remove your game statistics

Real Privacy Protection

Want actual privacy protection? Here's what really matters:

  • Use a VPN: Hide your real IP address from everyone
  • Tor Browser: Maximum anonymity for web browsing
  • Ad Blockers: Block tracking scripts from major tech companies
  • Fake Info: Use made-up names, emails, and details on sites that ask for them
  • Multiple Browsers: Separate browsing contexts prevent cross-site tracking

The Real Privacy Violators

The irony is thick: The same governments forcing us to show cookie notices are running massive surveillance programs. The same tech giants advocating for "privacy legislation" collect thousands of data points about you every day.

We're just a small gaming site. Google, Facebook, Amazon, and your government know more about you in one day than we'll learn in a lifetime.

Technical Details

  • Data Storage: Your game data is stored in JSON files on our servers
  • Data Retention: Game statistics are kept indefinitely unless you request deletion
  • Third Parties: We don't sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Security: Standard web security measures, but don't put sensitive info in your username!
