About This Project

ROFLFaucet's site income system supports our commitment to transparent charitable giving. Every month, we distribute available funds to verified charitable projects around the world.

How It Works:

  • Donations go directly to the ROFLFaucet charitable fund
  • At month-end, available funds are distributed to active projects
  • All transactions are publicly recorded for complete transparency
  • Recipients are verified through our project approval process

Recent Recipients Include:

  • Evans - Kenya Reforestation Project
  • Bitcoin4Ghana - Internet Connectivity Initiative
  • Additional verified projects as they become active

Transparency:

Every donation and distribution is recorded in our public transparency system. You can verify exactly where your contribution goes and see the real-world impact.
