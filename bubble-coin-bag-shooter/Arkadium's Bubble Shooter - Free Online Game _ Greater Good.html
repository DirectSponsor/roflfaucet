Arkadium's Bubble Shooter Overview

Match three or more bubbles of the same color to clear them and increase your score, but remember to bounce off the walls for hard-to-reach shots! By bursting lightning bubbles, you'll be able to clear entire rows. Also, keep an eye on how many bubbles you have left to make every shot count.
As you progress in Arkadium's Bubble Shooter, the bubble formations get more complex, making each level a new challenge. With a variety of bubble colors and power-ups, you'll have endless fun clearing the board. Keep an eye on your remaining moves to avoid getting stuck.
